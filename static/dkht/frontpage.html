Hydrotechnical Engineering

Engineering Consulting in water resources, value engineering.

I'm a registered professional mechanical engineer in British Columbia with ten years experience in hydropower, mining, and water resource management. My work experience includes technical engineering design (structural concrete and steel design, and hydraulic structures), quantitative modelling, hydrological analysis, value engineering, financial analysis, technical report writing, and management (project management, quality assurance, field supervision).
